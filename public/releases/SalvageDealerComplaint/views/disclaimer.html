Salvage Dealer Complaint

The Special Inspection Group (SIG) of the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) licenses and regulates salvage dealers pursuant to VA Code 46.2-1600. DMV also receives and investigates complaints from consumers/businesses regarding licensed dealers, unlicensed activity, and stolen vehicles.

In response to such complaints, DMV may encourage compliance with Virginia laws, pursue administrative actions, or refer the complaint to an appropriate agency for follow-up or enforcement action.

Use this form to file a complaint about a salvage dealer or suspected illegal activity. Submitting the form will trigger an email to SIG at enforcement@dmv.virginia.gov. regarding your complaint.
