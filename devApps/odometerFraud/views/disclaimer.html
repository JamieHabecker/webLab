Odometer Fraud

The Law Enforcement Division (LE) of the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) is responsible for investigating cases of odometer fraud. Odometer fraud can be defined as the disconnection, resetting or alteration of a motor vehicleâ€™s odometer with the intent to change the number of miles indicated.

Use this form to request that DMV LE investigate instances or suspected instances of motor vehicle odometer fraud, whether (1) you suspect that you are a victim of odometer fraud or (2) you are aware of an individual, business, or organization that knowingly engages in odometer fraud. Submitting the form will trigger an email to DMV LE at enforcement@dmv.virginia.gov.

All complaints are reviewed by DMV law enforcement. Some complaints are complex and may require several weeks or months to resolve.

Falsely reporting a crime is illegal and punishable as a misdemeanor (VA Code &18.2-461).
